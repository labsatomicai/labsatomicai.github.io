What is this?

AtomicAI is an independent AI project, it focus on open source software, scalability and quality. Our goal is to developt multiple tools that relies on artificial intelligence to provide the most accurate and functional predictions.

Development

All of our tools are written in Python, this is not a convention, C++ and JS are languages that we appreciate. Everything is being documented at our github, in the organization page you will find our projects, goals and some other information.
Since this is an open source project, feel free to help and contribute, any tip is welcome!
